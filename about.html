ABOUT THE ARTIST

Hello! I am Jamie Nguyen and I am currently pursuing a degree in Graphic Design. I am constantly challenging myself in learning a variety of new mediums to create unique works; I enjoy creating digital works as well as mixed media works. I have enjoyed creating things since I was young and love to create things for others. It brings me great pleasure seeing my artwork bring a smile to people's faces. So feel free to reach out to me if you're looking for something special.
